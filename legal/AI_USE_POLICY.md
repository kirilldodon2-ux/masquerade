# AI Use Policy — Masquerade Engine  
Owner: Borealis Labs (Kirill Dodon)  
Contact: hello@dodon.one

## 1. Purpose
Masquerade is a Fashion Intelligence Engine designed for stylistic research, outfit generation, try-on visualizations and creative workflows.

## 2. Prohibited Use
Users may NOT use the project to:
- generate realistic faces of real people,
- impersonate real individuals,
- create deceptive or fraudulent content,
- produce political, medical or harmful outputs,
- violate privacy or rights of publicity.

## 3. Allowed Use
✔ Fashion lookbooks  
✔ Item styling and outfit generation  
✔ Product cards and e-commerce imagery  
✔ Creative, editorial and atmospheric visuals  
✔ Movie/studio stylist pre-production (only fictional or consenting actors)

## 4. About Product Photography
Product-only images (clothes, objects, textiles) are allowed.  
Full-body models must be **fictional or anonymized**.

## 5. Data & Privacy
Masquerade does NOT store:
- user prompts,
- user images,
- generated content.

Logs contain only:
- timestamp,
- internal performance metrics,
- anonymized usage stats.

## 6. Responsibility
Users are responsible for complying with copyright, trademarks, and rights-of-publicity laws.

## 7. Updates
This policy may evolve as the project grows or moves to OpenAI Atlas.
